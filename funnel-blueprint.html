Big Redd | GoDer Stack Funnel Blueprint

Prepared: 2026-05-16 Audience tier: 82K IG (@networkgoder) + six-entity stack Core concept: Revival and rebuild of godermag.com + worldcbdexpo.com as front-end and back-end of one unified Big Redd brand funnel

The Problem This Blueprint Solves

Six businesses. Zero owned digital surfaces.

  • godermag.com: 301 redirect to @godermagazine IG. No website.
  • worldcbdexpo.com: GoDaddy parking page. No event infrastructure.
  • @networkgoder 82K: routes to Instagram bio, not to an owned asset.
  • Zero email list across all six entities.
  • Zero cross-sell between any of the six businesses.

Every dollar of advertiser, exhibitor, subscriber, and community revenue that should exist is blocked by the absence of basic web infrastructure.

Phase 1 - The Two Hubs (Days 1-7 from kickoff)

Hub A: GoDer Magazine at godermag.com

Purpose: Convert 18 years of brand equity into a real advertiser and reader funnel.

Page sections: 1. Hero - magazine masthead, current issue feature image, brand-feature application CTA 2. About GoDer - 18-year story, Bay Area culture coverage, network proof (Yukmouth, West Coast hip-hop, urban entertainment) 3. Featured coverage grid - 3-4 recent issue highlights with thumbnail + headline 4. Brand-feature application form - gated inquiry for premium placement 5. Advertiser pack - downloadable PDF (email-gated: captures advertiser-segment lead) 6. Event calendar - upcoming GoDer events, expo dates, Yukmouth Network appearances 7. The GoDer Drop opt-in - "Get the weekly drop: music, fashion, expo dates, network news" 8. Footer - all six entity links, contact, social stack

Revenue lines activated: - Brand-feature applications (email inquiry) - Advertiser pack downloads (email-gated lead capture) - The GoDer Drop subscribers (email list, magazine segment) - Event calendar driving expo page traffic

Timeline: Live in 7 days from kickoff.

Hub B: World CBD Expo at worldcbdexpo.com

Purpose: Take the parked domain and turn it into a live event-booking funnel.

Note: If the expo is currently on pause by design, this hub ships in dormant/waitlist mode. If the expo is active, it ships in full booking mode.

Page sections: 1. Hero - event name, next date / venue / city (or waitlist if paused), exhibitor-application CTA 2. Event story - history, mission, CBD industry positioning, Yukmouth Network connection 3. Exhibitor tier breakdown - booth sizes, prices, application link 4. Sponsor pack - downloadable PDF (email-gated) 5. Speaker roster - confirmed + placeholder (to be filled at kickoff) 6. Attendee ticket flow - Stripe-integrated purchase or waitlist 7. Returning-exhibitor wall - past exhibitor logos (to be added at kickoff) 8. The GoDer Drop opt-in - expo-segment version: "Get expo updates, dates, and early-bird booth availability" 9. Footer - full GoDer stack cross-links

Revenue lines activated: - Exhibitor applications (form inquiry or Stripe checkout) - Sponsor pack leads (email-gated download) - Attendee ticket sales (Stripe) - Expo-segment email subscribers

Timeline: Live in 7 days from kickoff.

Phase 2 - The Email List and Cross-Stack Router (Days 8-14)

The GoDer Drop

Weekly culture digest. Format: curated short-form email. New music picks, fashion editorial, expo update, Yukmouth Network feature, GoDer advertiser spotlight.

Opt-in entry points: - godermag.com (magazine segment) - worldcbdexpo.com (expo segment) - @networkgoder bio link - @godermagazine bio link - @world_cbd_expo bio link

Segment routing: - Magazine reader: gets issue highlights + brand-feature open slots + advertiser spotlights - Brand advertiser: gets rate card updates + brand-feature waitlist status + audience data drops - Expo attendee: gets event dates + early-bird booth pricing + speaker announcements - RE deal flow: gets iHeartRealEstate deal flow + network introductions

List projection: 3-7% IG-to-email over 6 months from 82K base = 2,500-5,700 subscribers. Year-1 revenue floor from list: $30K-$80K.

The Cross-Stack Router

Single bio link on @networkgoder that routes to a simple selector page (or directly to godermag.com as the primary hub). Footer links on all six entity pages cross-connect the whole stack.

Phase 3 - The GODER Network Community (Post-Install Scaling)

Format: Private community for operators in the Bay Area / West Coast urban entertainment, CBD, and RE space.

Offer: - $97/month founding rate - Capped at 250 founding members - Monthly live Q&A with Big Redd - Bi-weekly drop-in calls with rotating Yukmouth Network guests - Marketplace board: advertiser slots, sponsor opportunities, RE deal flow

Revenue projection: 250 members x $97/month = $291,000/year direct. Community becomes warm pipeline for premium expo booths and GoDer Magazine feature placements.

Gate: Phase 3 does not launch until Phase 1 and Phase 2 are live and the email list has at least 1,000 subscribers. The community needs the infrastructure to fill it.
